How does generator iteration work internally?

1. Generator function is called.
2. Generator object is created.
3. Code does not execute immediately.
4. next() starts execution.
5. yield pauses execution.
6. next() resumes execution.

Example:

def demo():
    print("Start")
    yield 1

    print("Middle")
    yield 2

    print("End")

gen = demo()

print(next(gen))
print(next(gen))
